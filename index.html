About...

My name is Aiden Nelson and I am a Computer Science undergraduate at Oregon State University. I created this website to showcase my hobby art.

When I set aside the time, I usually spend it on handbuilt ceramic projects. I am also a fan of glass fusion and doodling. Many of the projects that I have done with these crafts are displayed here, at AidensFineArt.com. I know the title is cheesy, I chose it because of that, because I like cheese.

I am by no means a pro, but I greatly enjoy practicing these hobbies. I wanted to create a place where I could look through the things I have done. If you're curious, feel free to take a look as well.
